Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25th April 2025

Thank you for using New Tab Countdown v2, with dark mode!

We care deeply about your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to any server or third-party service.

The countdowns you create are stored locally in your browser's storage (local storage) on your device. This means:

  • Your data stays private on your own device.
  • We have no access to your data.
  • No data is sent over the internet to us or any third parties.

How Your Data Is Used

  • The extension only uses your locally stored data to display the countdowns you have added.
  • The extension does not track your activity, behavior, or usage patterns.

Third-Party Services

New Tab Countdown v2, with dark mode does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

No cookies, trackers, or external scripts are embedded.

Your Control

  • You can add, edit, or delete countdowns at any time from within the extension interface.
  • Removing the extension from your browser will permanently delete all data stored by it.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page.

However, given that we do not collect or transmit data, changes are unlikely.
